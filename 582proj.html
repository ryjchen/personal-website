SI 582: Interaction Design Project

1. Problem Framing

College, for many people, is often the first time that young adults move away from the comfort of their home to being solely responsible for everything ranging from succeeding in academia to living on their own. It is already draining and stressful having to attend classes, complete homework and group projects, attend club meetings, etc. people often neglect the importance of eating a proper meal. Many does not cook at home because they find making meals from fresh ingredients to be too expensive, or they simply do not know how to cook.

Target Audience

Here in Ann Arbor, it is a college town filled with predominantly students attending University of Michigan. My primary target audience is the students who do not have any prior knowledge in cooking. My secondary target audience is the students who know the basics of cooking, but would like to find recipes that are fast to make and ingredients that would not break the bank.

2. Research

Design Philosophy

My goal is to design and create a mobile application or similar physical infrastructure that provides an outlet for students to learn the basics of cooking and eating healthy. I hope to design an easily accessible solution that is simple to use yet effective.
